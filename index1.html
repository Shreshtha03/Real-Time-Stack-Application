How It Works

Welcome to our real-time stack demonstration. This application simulates the behavior of a stack data structure, where you can push and pop items in real time.

Stack Structure

A stack is a data structure that follows the Last-In, First-Out (LIFO) principle. Imagine it as a stack of books, where the most recently added book is on top, and you can only add or remove books from the top of the stack.
